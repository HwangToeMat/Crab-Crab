# Crab Infinity - Design Tokens & Visual Identity

## 1. Concept: "Infinite Intelligence & Biological Evolution"
- **Visual Style**: Glassmorphism + Neo-Brutalism for a high-tech yet organic feel.
- **Evolution Metaphor**: The "Crab" symbol evolves from a simple pixel-art egg to a detailed, high-resolution mecha-crab (v2.0).

## 2. Color Palette (Design Tokens)
- `--bg-dark`: #0b0d17 (Deep Space)
- `--primary`: #7b2cbf (Electric Purple - AI Intelligence)
- `--secondary`: #00b4d8 (Deep Blue - Reliability & Growth)
- `--accent`: #f72585 (Neon Pink - High Energy/Achievement)
- `--glass-bg`: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.05)
- `--glass-border`: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)

## 3. Typography
- **Headings**: 'Space Grotesk' or sans-serif (Bold, wide letter spacing)
- **Body**: 'Inter' or sans-serif (Clean, high readability)

## 4. Components
- **The Evolution Chamber (Avatar View)**: A central glass container where the "Crab" evolves.
- **The Infinity Loop (Progress Bar)**: A circular progress bar indicating XP progress toward the next evolution stage.
- **Gemini Insight Bubble**: Floating cards for AI coaching suggestions.

## 5. Evolution Stages
1. **The Core (Stage 0)**: A pulsing purple orb.
2. **The Hatchling (Stage 1)**: Small, cute, semi-transparent.
3. **The Warrior (Stage 2)**: Armor-plated, strong claws.
4. **The Infinity King (Stage 3)**: Majestic, glowing with AI energy.
