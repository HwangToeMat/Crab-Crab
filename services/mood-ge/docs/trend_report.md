# Trend Analysis Report: Burnout Recovery & Ritual Culture

## 1. Market Context (South Korea)
- **High Stress Society**: The prevalence of 'Burnout' among Gen Z and Millennials is at an all-time high, driven by competitive environments and social media fatigue.
- **Rise of 'Ritual'**: There is a growing movement of young Koreans adopting "Rituals" (daily small routines) to find stability and meaning in their lives.
- **Digital Mental Care**: Increased demand for non-clinical, approachable mental health apps that don't feel like "work."

## 2. Identified Trend
- **"Micro-Challenge for Small Success"**: Users prefer low-commitment, high-frequency wins (e.g., '1-minute stretching', 'Looking at the sky') over long-term, difficult goals.
- **Emotional Visualization**: Gamifying emotional states through character growth is highly effective for retention in the Korean app market.

## 3. Opportunity
- Combining habit tracking with emotional logging through a friendly character (The "Crab").
- Providing a "Burnout Recovery Score" to give users a sense of progress.
