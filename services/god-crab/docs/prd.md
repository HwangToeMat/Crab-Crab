# PRD: God-Crab (갓게) - Your Godsaeng Companion

## 1. Core Value Proposition
"Small habits, Big Crab." Help users achieve their "Godsaeng" goals through gamified habit tracking and community support, featuring the evolving Crab character.

## 2. Target Audience
-   Students and young professionals (MZ Generation) in Korea.
-   People who want to build daily routines but lack motivation.
-   Individuals who enjoy gamified experiences and sharing progress.

## 3. Key Features (MVP)
-   **Crab Habit Tracker**: Add daily tasks (e.g., "Wake up at 6 AM," "Study 1 hour").
-   **Crab Evolution**: Your Crab character grows, changes color, or gets accessories as you complete tasks.
-   **Godsaeng Dashboard**: Visual representation of your consistency (streaks, charts).
-   **Crab-munity (Micro-sharing)**: Share your "Godsaeng" status with a small group of friends or locally.
-   **Habit Templates**: One-tap additions of popular Korean habits.

## 4. User Personas
-   **Min-ji (24, University Student)**: Wants to prepare for exams consistently but gets distracted easily. Needs a "fun" reason to check her to-do list.
-   **Ji-hun (31, New Office Worker)**: Wants to maintain a healthy lifestyle after work. Uses the app to track his "Miracle Morning" routine.

## 5. Success Metrics
-   Daily Active Users (DAU).
-   Average Habit Completion Rate.
-   Retention Rate (14-day, 30-day).
