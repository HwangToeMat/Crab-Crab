# Crab Infinity (무한게) - Trend Analysis Report

## 1. Market Context & Trend Identification
- **Self-Evolution Trend**: South Korea's "God-saeng" (God-life) trend continues to evolve into specific "Micro-habits" and "Skill Stacking."
- **AI Personalization**: Users no longer want general advice; they seek hyper-personalized coaching that adapts to their daily progress.
- **Gamification of Life**: The boundary between gaming and life is blurring, with users seeking XP (Experience Points) and Level-up systems for real-world tasks.

## 2. Pain Points
- **Plan Fatigue**: People create great plans but fail due to lack of immediate feedback and adaptation.
- **Inconsistency**: Maintaining a habit for more than 2 weeks is the primary hurdle for 80% of users.
- **Feedback Gap**: Traditional planners are static; they don't "respond" or "evolve" with the user.

## 3. Opportunity
- **Gemini-Powered Evolution**: Using LLMs to analyze user progress (input/logs) and dynamically update their "Evolution Roadmap."
- **Dynamic Reward System**: A visual "Crab Evolution" metaphor that changes its appearance based on user achievement.

## 4. Conclusion
A service that doesn't just "track" but "evolves" alongside the user is highly marketable in the current "Skill Stacking" and "AI Agent" era.
