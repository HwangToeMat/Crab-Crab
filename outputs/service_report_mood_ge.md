# Final Report: Mood-Ge (무드게) - Burnout Recovery Service

## 1. Service Vision & Goals
**Mood-Ge** is a hyper-local emotional wellness service designed for busy professionals and students in South Korea. The goal is to provide a low-barrier, gamified environment for burnout recovery through daily mood logging and micro-challenges.

- **Vision**: "Burnout recovery, one small crab at a time."
- **Goal**: Achieve daily engagement through 'Small Success' (소확행) rituals.

## 2. Technical Architecture
- **Frontend**: React-inspired SPA with Vanilla JS and CSS for maximum compatibility and performance.
- **Backend**: FastAPI (Python) for rapid API responses and scalable micro-services.
- **Data**: SQLite/JSON-based persistent storage.
- **Visuals**: CSS-based character animation and responsive design tokens.

## 3. Key Features (Evolved)
- **AI Micro-Challenge Recommendation**: Personalized challenges based on user mood (Happy, Tired, Sad).
- **Crab Cheer Community**: Anonymous peer-to-peer support system with a "Cheer" counter.
- **Crab Leveling & Evolution**: Gamified progression system where points lead to character growth.
- **Daily Mood Logging**: Emotional check-in with character reactions and optional notes.
- **History & Weekly Report**: Data visualization for long-term burnout tracking.

## 4. Evolution Loop Summary (10-Cycle Upgrade)
Through 10 cycles of evolution supervised by the **Evolution Director**, Mood-Ge has transformed from a simple tracker to a community-driven wellness ecosystem:
- **Phase 1 (Cycle 1-3)**: Implemented AI Recommendation and Community Foundations.
- **Phase 2 (Cycle 4-7)**: Added Gamification (Level/Points) and Micro-Interactions.
- **Phase 3 (Cycle 8-10)**: Optimized Mobile UI, SEO sharing, and Accessibility.

## 5. Local Setup & Test Guide
1. **Backend**: 
   - `cd services/mood-ge/backend`
   - `pip install -r ../requirements.txt`
   - `python main.py`
2. **Frontend**:
   - Open `services/mood-ge/frontend/index.html` in any modern browser.

## 6. Market Success Potential
Mood-Ge addresses the "Mental Wellness" trend in Korea's Gen Z/Millennials. The friendly "Crab" character provides emotional support without the clinical weight of traditional therapy apps. Its focus on "Micro-Challenges" aligns with the high demand for ritual-based self-care.
